Imagery

Editorial illustration in the spirit of HBR, The Atlantic, or Stripe Press — flat, painterly, calm. Cyan-dominant with one warm accent. No text, no UI, no “brain + circuit” clichés.

Locked: flat painterly style, soft single-source lighting (varied per image), cyan/sky palette + one warm accent, calm and intentional mood, never photoreal or 3D. Varies: composition — metaphor, tableau, single person, or pair — chosen to fit the argument.
