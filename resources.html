Frequently Asked Questions

AI is a tool that amplifies human capability, not a replacement. Our approach emphasizes partnership.AI handles repetitive, time-consuming tasks while humans focus on strategy, creativity, and decision-making. The real question isn't whether AI will replace you, but whether you'll use it to become more valuable in your role.

Your role evolves, it doesn't disappear. With AI handling execution, your value shifts to higher-level thinking: strategy, relationship-building, and judgment calls. A personal AI agent serves as your bridge during this transition, handling your routine work while you focus on what only you can do.

If your team spends time on repetitive tasks, handles high volume, or struggles with scaling processes, AI implementation can help. We start with an assessment phase to understand your workflows, identify high-impact opportunities, and determine if AI is the right fit for your specific needs.

Timeline depends on complexity and scope. A simple AI agent might be ready in weeks. A comprehensive strategy spanning your entire team could take 3-6 months. We follow our proven 5-step process: Assess, Strategy, Build, Test, and Amplify.
