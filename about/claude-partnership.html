Partner Network

Our partnership with Claude

GrowthMax is a proud member of the Claude Partner Network. We build custom AI agents on Anthropic's Claude — the same principle we bring to people: partnership, not replacement.

What this partnership means

The Claude Partner Network is Anthropic's program for companies that build with Claude, Anthropic's family of AI models. Our membership means the agents we build for you run on frontier models designed around safety, reliability, and genuine usefulness — and that we work close to the platform as it evolves.

For you, that translates into something simple: the AI partner we build sits on a foundation we trust, backed by a company whose values line up with ours. We don't bolt AI onto your workflow for novelty. We build agents that augment your expertise, amplify your judgment, and hold up under real work.

Why we build on Claude

The model you build on shapes the partner your team works with every day. Here's why Claude is ours.

Built for judgment

Claude is strong at nuanced reasoning, long context, and careful work — the qualities that let an agent support expert decisions rather than just automate clicks.

Safety by design

Anthropic builds Claude with safety and reliability at the core. When an agent acts on your behalf, that foundation matters — for trust, for compliance, and for peace of mind.

Aligned values

Partnership, not replacement isn't just our tagline — it's how we read the whole field. Building on Claude keeps us close to a company that treats AI as a partner to human work.

What it means for your project

Custom agents built on a frontier model, matched to your role and workflows — not a generic chatbot.

A practitioner who stays current with Claude's capabilities as the platform evolves.

Training and adoption support so your team works with the agent, building the judgment to get the most from it.
