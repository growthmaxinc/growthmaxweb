## Company Overview

### Mission & Core Philosophy
GrowthMax builds custom AI agents designed for **Partnership, Not Replacement**. We help organizations and individuals leverage AI to augment expertise, amplify judgment, and achieve outcomes that neither humans nor machines could accomplish alone.

Organizations are adopting AI. People want to use it. But there's confusion at both organizational and individual levels:
- Teams are scattered across fragmented tools
- Leadership lacks a coherent AI adoption strategy
- Individuals are asking: "Where do I fit?"

**The gap isn't resistance. It's disorientation while culture lags behind technology.**

### Our Solution
Personal AI agents that:
- Give teams coherence and structure today
- Provide one focused expert to work with
- Show employees their expertise is amplified, not displaced
- Serve as a practical interim bridge while organizations evolve culture, skills, and infrastructure

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will AI replace my job?
No. We believe in partnership, not replacement. Our custom AI agents are designed to augment your expertise and amplify your judgment. Your role evolves to incorporate AI as a powerful tool, making you more effective—not obsolete.

### Where do I fit in an AI-driven future?
This is exactly what we help organizations answer. Your expertise becomes more valuable because you're directing an AI agent. You focus on judgment, strategy, and human-centric decisions while the agent handles execution and analysis. You're not replaced—you're amplified.

### Is AI implementation right for our organization?
If you have specific roles where expertise could be amplified, or teams ready to build AI capability, yes. We assess your needs and recommend the right solution: Custom Agent Development, AI Engineer Bootcamp, or AI Foundations Bootcamp.

### How long does AI implementation take?
Custom AI Agent Development typically follows a structured process: Assessment (1-2 weeks), Strategy (1-2 weeks), Build (2-4 weeks), Test & Iterate (2-4 weeks), Amplify (ongoing). Timeline varies based on complexity. Bootcamps range from 4-12 weeks depending on curriculum depth.

### What makes GrowthMax different?
- **Philosophy:** Partnership, not replacement—we reshape how organizations think about AI
- **Founders:** 20+ years of proven experience building, scaling, and architecting
- **Approach:** Tailored solutions (custom agents, engineer training, organizational literacy)
- **Results:** We've seen firsthand how custom AI agents transform organizational evolution

### Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?
Yes. Our Amplify phase is ongoing. We monitor performance, optimize, and scale with your organization. Your agent grows as you grow.

### What's the cost?
Pricing varies based on solution:
- **Custom AI Agent Development:** Custom quote based on scope and complexity
- **AI Engineer Bootcamp:** Custom pricing based on team size and duration
- **AI Foundations Bootcamp:** Custom pricing based on team size and organizational scope

For specifics, reach out at hi@growthmaxinc.com

### Can we build AI agents in-house instead?
You can, but consider the time investment, talent acquisition, and ongoing maintenance. GrowthMax accelerates your time-to-value, leverages our expertise, and provides ongoing optimization. We're a partnership to help you move faster.

### Scenario 1: Organization exploring AI
**User:** "We're thinking about AI but not sure where to start"

**Response approach:**
1. Validate the concern (disorientation is real)
2. Explain the three options (assess which fits)
3. Recommend starting with assessment or foundations bootcamp
4. Offer next step: call with Chris/team

### Scenario 2: Individual worried about replacement
**User:** "Will AI take my job?"

**Response approach:**
1. Directly address: "No, but your role will evolve"
2. Reframe: "Your expertise becomes more valuable, not less"
3. Example: "You direct the AI; you make judgment calls; you focus on strategy"

1. Validate progress: "That's great you're moving forward"
