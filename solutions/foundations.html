AI Adoption Starts With Understanding

AI adoption doesn't start in engineering. It starts with understanding. Our AI Foundations Bootcamp enables cross-functional teams (product, ops, support, leadership) to see AI not as a tool to automate humans away, but as a force multiplier.

A partner that elevates your judgment, unlocks new possibilities, and drives organizational excellence.

Who Should Attend

  • Product managers planning AI initiatives
  • Operations and support leaders implementing AI
  • Executive and leadership teams driving adoption
  • Anyone across your organization wanting AI fluency
  • Cross-functional teams collaborating on AI projects

What Participants Will Learn

  • AI fundamentals and how LLMs work
  • Partnership-based AI implementation
  • How AI changes roles and responsibilities
  • Leadership and change management for AI adoption
  • Strategic thinking about AI deployment
  • Overcoming AI adoption barriers

Learning Outcomes

Participants emerge with clear understanding of AI's potential, how to implement it in partnership, and strategies for ensuring adoption across their organization without fear or resistance.
